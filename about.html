Our Story

Setting the Standard in Luxury Real Estate

Founded in 2009 by Jonathan Prestige, our firm was born from a simple yet ambitious vision: to redefine the luxury real estate experience by combining unparalleled market expertise with deeply personalized service.

What began as a boutique practice in Beverly Hills has grown into one of the most respected names in luxury real estate, serving discerning clients across the nation's most coveted markets — from Manhattan to Malibu, Aspen to Miami.

Our success is built on a foundation of discretion, integrity, and an unwavering commitment to excellence. Every property we represent is carefully vetted, every client relationship cultivated with the utmost care, and every transaction executed with precision.
